## Who We Are

AITools is a review site built by solopreneurs, for solopreneurs. We test AI tools so you don't waste time and money on the wrong ones.

## How We Review

Every tool on this site has been used in real projects. We don't do sponsored reviews. We don't copy press releases. We sit down, use the tool for at least a week, and write about what actually happened.

Our reviews cover:
- **What it does** — plain language, no marketing speak
- **How it feels** — the real UX, not the feature list
- **What it costs** — pricing breakdown, hidden costs, free tier limits
- **Who it's for** — is this tool right for a one-person business?

## Scoring

We rate tools on three dimensions:
1. **Ease of Use** — How fast can you go from zero to productive?
2. **Value for Money** — Does the price justify what you get?
3. **Solopreneur Fit** — Built for teams of one, or awkwardly repurposed?

## Editorial Independence

- No paid reviews, ever
- No affiliate links that influence our ratings
- We buy our own subscriptions
- If a tool sucks, we say so
